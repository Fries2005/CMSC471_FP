CMSC 471 Final Project

Decoding Music Genre Through Audio + Lyrics

Can we reliably define and differentiate musical genres using analytical methods such as audio features, lyrical density, and structural song data?

Why Visualize This?

Humans often classify songs by feel, rhythm, and familiarity. Translating those subjective signals into objective metrics is difficult at scale, especially across many genres and thousands of tracks.

This project uses a sequence of visualizations in storytelling format to surface patterns between genre and variables like tempo, energy, loudness, key, structure, and lyrical usage. The chart below is one module in that broader narrative.

Cluster Lyrical DNA

Activate Brush Select in the t-SNE chart to the right, then drag a rectangle over any region of the t-SNE. The words that define that spatial cluster, regardless of genre, will appear here. Cross-genre clusters reveal lyrical themes that unite acoustically similar songs.

t-SNE Genre Clustering

Here, each song, represented by a dots, is projected onto a 2D space based on how musically similar they are to one another. We use t-SNE to project 9 separate Spotify metrics, shown by clicking any dot, on the 2D plane. Songs that are more alike appear closer together, while songs that are different appear further apart. See basic information about a song by hovering over a dot, and click on a dot to learn more about a song and hear a preview! We show information such as the 9 different Spotify metrics in a radar chart, key stats, common lyrics of the song, and similar songs via Nearest Neighbors. You can also move around the map by clicking, dragging, and pinching to zoom in or out. You can also use the legend to toggle different genres on or off, either isolating specific styles or comparing cross-genre in the feature space.

Draw a box to reveal the lyrical DNA of any cluster

Lyrical DNA by Genre

This visualization uses word clouds to organize common lyrics by genre. Each genre, identical to the t-SNE visualization, has its own word cloud with words extracted from its song lyrics. The size of words are related to their frequency of songs in that genre, with higher frequency words being larger in the word cloud. Use this part of the visualization to learn more about the language that goes into defining a genre.

Clicking on a word in the world cloud highlights all the songs in the t-SNE cluster from that genre that contain the word. You are also able to click on the genre title in the top left of the word cloud to also isolate that genre in the t-SNE cluster, similar to selecting the genre title in the legend. This interaction with the t-SNE cluster can help compare across genre and learn where genres align and disagree on lyricism.

Audio Feature Fingerprints by Genre

Average radar shape for each genre using the same 9 Spotify metrics from the t-SNE visualization. Compare how EDM peaks on energy & danceability, country on acousticness & valence, and rap on speechiness at a glance. Click any card to isolate that genre across all views. Hover for exact percentages.

Feature Distributions by Genre

Select any audio feature from the 9 Spotify metrics to see how it distributes across genres. The violin shape shows the full density of values. the box marks the interquartile range, the white line is the median, and the dot is the mean. Wide violins means blurry genres. Narrow, peaked shapes means pure genres for that feature.
