CMSC 471 Final Project

Decoding Music Genre Through Audio + Lyrics

Can we reliably define and differentiate musical genres using analytical methods such as audio features, lyrical density, and structural song data?

Why Visualize This?

Humans often classify songs by feel, rhythm, and familiarity. Translating those subjective signals into objective metrics is difficult at scale, especially across many genres and thousands of tracks.

This project uses a sequence of visualizations in storytelling format to surface patterns between genre and variables like tempo, energy, loudness, key, structure, and lyrical usage. The chart below is one module in that broader narrative.

t-SNE Genre Clustering

Songs projected into 2D space by feature similarity. Click any dot to explore that song. Use zoom, pan, and click legend genres to filter.

Lyrical DNA by Genre

Top words from song lyrics for each genre. Click a word to highlight matching songs in the t-SNE. Click a genre label to isolate it.
